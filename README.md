# lrc-progressbar-minigame
A standalone RedM progress bar resource. 

Includes a standard progress bar and a timing minigame (press E in the zone).

Both exports are synchronous — they block until the player finishes or fails.

Exports: StartProgress · StartTimingMinigame · cancelCurrentProgressBar · initiate

Configure defaults in config.lua. All export params are optional and fall back to config values.

Free to use. Do not redistribute as your own.
